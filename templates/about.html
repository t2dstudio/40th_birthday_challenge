About Us

This why we do what we do

A period of forty always usher in a time of revival and rebirth. it always determines the realization of identity of a person and nation; For this reason we have chosen to make it loud on the occasion of our 40th birthday celebration

We have enjoyed grace to witness forty years of purposeful living, unquantifiable measures of favour, uncompromising love of God.

We have in forty years met numerous wonderful souls that had in no measure contributed to our social existence,earthly living, experiential growth, progressive discoveries either through being a family member, friend, colleague, covenant brother and sister, teacher, instructors, father, mother, etc

As we commend your manifold contributions, we request that you join us in celebrating this birthday week in a way most desirous to us

We have elected to celebrate this day with you in mind; and we therefore invite you to be a part of this celebrations by walking with us through the path that spreads more love to our world.

We are by this notice humbly requesting that our friend and family share the love of Jesus Christ by giving $10 or N1,000 Naira to anyone you have not met before nor seen for over three(3) months.

Thank You


Taiwo & Kehinde Adeoye
